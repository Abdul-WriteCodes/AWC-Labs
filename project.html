Vitalyx-GPT

Vitalyx-GPT is being developed as an intelligent system for tracking and analyzing chronic indicators of cardiometabolic disorders, including obesity, diabetes, and hypertension.Vitalyx-GPT is to be powered by rule-based heuristics and large language model (LLM) to analyze health data of patients, alert any detected cardiometabolic disorder risk patterns, and provide vital conversational health support for patients.

EcoBudget

EcoBudget is an AI-powered personal carbon pricing and environmental cost estimator. It enables users to estimate the carbon footprint and environmental costs associated with energy consumption across various areas, including home and office electricity, transportation, heating and cooling, and cooking fuels.
Designed with sustainability in mind, EcoBudget helps individuals make more environmentally conscious decisions

Alchemia-GPT

Alchemia-GPT is an intelligent system powered by large language model (LLM), designed to assist, enhance, and streamline the drug design and development process. It suggests potential drug targets or candidates, proposes novel molecules, evaluates their properties, and recommends synthetic routes.
Alchemia-GPT serves as a one-stop intelligent solution for researchers and pharmaceutical companies aiming to accelerate innovation in drug discovery.

ForekastAI

ForekastAI is a machine learning-powered asset valuation and future worth estimation system. It analyzes the current value of assets, performs scenario analysis, and predicts future worth using historical trends and economic indicators.
ForekastAI is tailored for asset and investment management firms seeking data-driven insights for strategic decision-making
